The Forbidden List

To ensure maximum trust, we explicitly forbid our app (and our developers) from engaging in the following behaviors. This is our promise to you:

  • NO Keylogging of passwords, banking credentials, or private credentials.
  • NO Selling of user data to third-party data brokers.
  • NO Hidden background audio recording or camera usage.
  • NO Uploading of your contacts list to our servers.
  • NO Scanning your files or photos without your explicit action (like "Scan Image").
  • NO Preventing you from uninstalling or disabling the app.
  • NO Misleading users about what the Accessibility Service is doing.
  • NO Continuous background operation without user-initiated triggers.

How to Verify?

Transparency is meaningless without verification. We encourage technical users to verify these claims:

  • Network Traffic Analysis: Run a proxy (like Wireshark or PCAPdroid) to see that we only communicate with the AI provider endpoints you expect (e.g., Google Gemini API, OpenAI API).
  • Permission Settings: Check your Android Settings > Privacy > Permission Manager to verify we never ask for Camera or Microphone unless prompted.
