Our Core Philosophy

Aido is built differently. In an era of data harvesting, we chose a harder path: Local-First, Transparent-By-Design, and User-Controlled.

We are developers who use this app daily. We wouldn't build something we wouldn't trust with our own personal data. This Transparency Center lists exactly how our app works, down to the code logic level.

The 3 Pillars of Aido Trust

  • Right to Know: You deserve to know exactly what code runs on your device.
  • Right to Choose: Every major feature has a toggle. You are in control.
  • Right to Privacy: Your keystrokes and data are yours. We have zero interest in them.
