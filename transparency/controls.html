Kill Switches

Aido provides multiple layers of "off" switches for your peace of mind.

Master Toggle

Inside the app, a prominent "Enable Aido" switch instantly freezes all background services. When off, Aido is functionally dead.

Notification Action

A persistent notification allows you to "Pause" or "Stop" the service instantly without opening the app.

The Blacklist

Don't want Aido reading your Signal messages or banking app? No problem.

Go to Settings > Disabled Apps (Blacklist). Select any installed app. Aido's code (`disabledApps`) will rigorously ignore every event coming from that package name. It effectively blinds itself to that specific app.

Final Exit

Aido is a standard Android app. It does not install as a "Device Administrator" or rootkit. You can uninstall it via the Play Store or standard Settings menu in seconds, wiping all local data immediately.
