Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our compliance with children's online privacy protection regulations.

Age Restriction

Aido is intended for users aged 13 and older.

The app is not directed at children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

COPPA Compliance

Aido complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by:

  • No Child-Directed Content: The app is not designed or marketed to children
  • No Data Collection from Children: We do not knowingly collect personal information from users under 13
  • Age-Appropriate Design: The app includes features that requi mature understanding (AI prompting, technical settings)
  • No Registration Required: We don't collect birthdates or age information during setup

Parental Notice

For Parents and Guardians

If you believe your child under 13 has used Aido and you wish to:

  • Remove the app from their device
  • Delete any local data
  • Report concerns

Please contact us immediately at aiqknow@gmail.com with "COPPA" in the subject line.

What Data Could Be Collected

If a child under 13 were to use Aido (which violates our terms), the following local data could be stored on their device:

Data Type Storage Location How to Delete
Text prompts sent to AI Not stored by us (sent directly to AI providers) Contact AI provider
Clipboard history Local device only (encrypted) Uninstall app or clear data
Saved prompts Local device only Uninstall app or clear data
App settings Local device only Uninstall app or clear data

Important: Since all data is stored locally and we don't operate servers collecting user data, uninstalling the app immediately removes all traces.

Verifiable Parental Consent

Aido does not require parental consent because:

  • The app is not directed at children under 13
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone (including children)
  • There is no user registration or account creation
  • All data is stored locally, not sent to our servers

Age-Appropriate Design Code (UK)

For UK users, Aido follows the Age-Appropriate Design Code principles:

  • Privacy by Default: No data collection without explicit user action
  • No Profiling: We don't create profiles or track behavior
  • Transparency: This entire Transparency Center provides clear information
  • Minimal Data Collection: Only what's necessary for functionality
  • No Nudge Techniques: No manipulative design patterns

If We Discover Underage Use

What we would do:

  1. Immediately investigate the report
  2. Contact the parent/guardian (if contact info is available)
  3. Provide instructions for app removal and data deletion
  4. Since we don't collect server-side data, no remote deletion is necessary
  5. Document the incident internally for compliance records

Contact for COPPA Concerns

If you have questions or concerns about children's privacy:

Email: aiqknow@gmail.com
Subject Line: "COPPA Inquiry" or "Children's Privacy"

We will respond within 48 hours to any children's privacy concerns.
