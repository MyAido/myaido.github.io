Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Aido ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Aido is designed with privacy as a priority. We collect minimal data necessary for the app's functionality:

  • Accessibility Data: We use Accessibility Services to read text on your screen ONLY when you explicitly trigger the AI assistant. We do not store or transmit this data to our servers.
  • API Usage: If you use our default AI service, anonymized prompts are sent to our AI providers (like OpenAI/Google) solely for generating responses.
  • Device Info: Basic device information for crash reporting and analytics (optional).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the AI writing features.
  • Fix bugs and optimize performance.
  • Respond to customer service requests.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, please also remember that we cannot guarantee that the internet itself is 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this policy, you may email us at support@aido.app.
