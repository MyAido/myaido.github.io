MIUI (Xiaomi's software) aggressively kills background apps to save battery. This creates issues for accessibility services like Aido.

Solution Checklist

Please follow every step below to ensure the app works correctly.

1. Disable Battery Optimization ‚ö°

Go to Settings > Apps > Manage apps > Aido > Battery saver and select "No restrictions".

2. Enable Autostart üöÄ

Open the Security app > Permissions > Autostart. Find Aido and toggle it ON.

3. Allow Background Permissions üîì

Go to Settings > Apps > Manage apps > Aido > Other permissions. Enable "Display pop-up windows" and "Start in background".

4. Lock App in Recents üîí

Open Recent Apps menu. Long press (or drag down) on the Aido card until you see a lock icon. Tap it to lock the app in memory.

5. Disable MIUI Optimization (Advanced) ‚öôÔ∏è

Enable Developer Options (tap MIUI version 7 times in About Phone). Then go to Developer Options and turn OFF "MIUI Optimization".

Still facing issues? Try restarting your device after applying these settings.
